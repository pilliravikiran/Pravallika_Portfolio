✱ About me

I lead with design, ship with engineering, and keep both halves of the craft in conversation.

Where design thinking meets engineering discipline.

My career began in 2021 at Sails Software Solutions in India, right after my B.Tech at IIIT Nuzvid. There I built the Angular frontend for a pharmacy management platform — contributing to a reusable component library, real-time data updates, page performance improvements, and modernization of legacy modules across multiple release cycles.

In August 2023 I moved to the United States to pursue a Master's in Computer Science at Sacred Heart University, graduating in December 2024 with a 3.9 GPA — deepening my foundations in software engineering, cloud computing, and system design.

In April 2025 I joined Redsails Technologies (formerly known as PrimeRx) in New York, where I expanded from pure frontend craft into full-stack delivery — now owning .NET Core API development, SQL Server tuning, Azure Blob integration, and AI-assisted workflows alongside Angular UI work. Today I lead end-to-end feature delivery across our pharmacy cloud platform and patient engagement product, contributing to secure messaging with media and history support, cross-platform transaction flows, and enhancements across multiple modules including facility management, compounding RX, and 340B program support.

On the side, I built an AI-powered quiz platform from scratch out of personal interest — taking it from idea to live deployment in just 2 days using AI-assisted development. It hosted live interactive sessions for 300+ participants with real-time leaderboards, animated content, and team-based gameplay.
