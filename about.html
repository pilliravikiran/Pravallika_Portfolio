About

My career began as a UI/UX Developer building user-facing experiences for a pharmacy management platform — contributing to shared component systems, performance improvements, and modernization of legacy modules across multiple release cycles.

After completing my Master's in Computer Science at Sacred Heart University with a 3.9 GPA, I expanded into full-stack delivery — now owning .NET Core API development, SQL Server tuning, Azure Blob Storage integration, and AI-assisted workflows alongside Angular UI work.

What draws me to this craft is the chance to bring a combined design and engineering perspective to products that demand both polish and scale. I treat accessibility, performance, and visual hierarchy as production concerns rather than afterthoughts — and I use AI tools throughout the development lifecycle to deliver faster without compromising quality.
